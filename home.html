About

What set's a Jakub Jaunt from other New York City Walking tours? Great question! A native New Yorker from when Peter Stuyvesant was governor, I've walked nearly every single street in this city and taken in every sight and smell of rotten garbage it can offer. After graduating with a Master's in history I decided to start my company to tell the stories that I think people are interested in. Every brick, lamp post, nook, cranney, and cockroach has a story and I'm just looking to tell it. When I'm not guiding I can be found, biking down the Hudson River bike path, enjoying a great dollar slice of pizza, or taking in the views of the city from the sail boat I volunteer on.
